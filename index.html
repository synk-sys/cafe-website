Our Story

Born from two worlds,
brewed with love.

Café 2nd Gen takes its name from the experience of being a second-generation immigrant — the child of cultures, bridging heritage and modernity in every cup.

What started as a humble summer kiosk at Quartier Dix-30 has blossomed into a beloved Griffintown destination at 717 Rue William. Our drinks fuse Vietnamese coffee tradition with playful global twists — ube, matcha, knafeh, tahini — because belonging is never just one thing.
